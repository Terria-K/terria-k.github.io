Starcourse Dock is a content mods for [Cobalt Core](https://braceyourselfgames.com/cobalt-core/) that adds 8 new ships to the game. All ships has its own gimmicks that may have
an advantages and disadvantages.

**Requirements:**
+ This mod uses [Nickel](https://github.com/Shockah/Nickel) a mod loader for Cobalt Core and it is required to function.
+ This mod also uses [Kokoro](https://www.nexusmods.com/cobaltcore/mods/4) which is a utility mod and it is required to function as well.

### Download Starcourse Dock

# Albireo
Albireo is a ship that comes with two decks that swaps everytime the deck shuffles. The linked cards are randomized and can be obtained from card offering,
and starter cards. The ship designed is intended to be similar to Artemis.
<Image src={Albireo} alt="albireo-gameplay"/>

# Alpherg
Alpherg is an aquatic lifeform creature that shoots from its empty space, and dances when using an attack actions. The dance mechanic just moves the
empty space from the innermost left to the innermost right, and vice versa, making it similar to Ares and Gemini.
<Image src={Alpherg} alt="alpherg-gameplay"/>

# Atlas
Atlas is the last ship that has been added to the mod, and it moves by playing a card, it moves equal distance from the center of your hand of that card,
making it the powerful ship of this mod, but also has the least amount of health and max shield. This ship is similar to Tiderunner.
<Image src={Atlas} alt="atlas-gameplay"/>

# Charon
Charon is a merciless ship that focus on just attacking. This ship is inspired on the drill enemies, and what it does is to destroy
the enemy as fast as it can by generating charges from its attacks. This ship also damages itself when the charges are too much which makes it risky to play
as well.
<Image src={Charon} alt="charon-gameplay"/>

# Io
Io is a midrow-focused ship that has two missile bays to its left and right. It can launch two Io Drones once which is the main source of damage. But, if
any midrows are destroyed, the ship will pull it and became a card that can be launch on one side only, or it can just be removed.
<Image src={Io} alt="io-gameplay"/>

# Sirius
Sirius is a four-wide ship with two missile bays which are manually toggled, and launches dual drone that copies player's attacks. Due to it launching a dual drone,
this ship has became the hardest ship to play on this mod with its being low on health.
<Image src={Sirius} alt="sirius-gameplay"/>

# Spica
Spica is an everyone's favorite ship*, and it is also the first ship that Starcourse Dock. The ship is unable to move on its own and
requires instant move for it to move. The evade movement moves the cannon instead of the ship, and it does have an empty space evading attacks are still possible.
The ship also has an ability to shrink to avoid attacks from its edge, and to reduce damages from its armored wings.

> *on this mod by the way.

<Image src={Spica} alt="spica-gameplay"/>

# Betelgeuse
Betelgeuse is a powerful 6-wide ship that has 3 missile launchers which are activated by being hit, and launch the missile by overheating. 
Due to missile bay and cannon placement, Betelgeuse is recommended to launch the 3 missiles that deals 4 damages to the enemy instead. The missile damage cannot be boosted, 
but it does provides a defense by being an empty space for one turn.
<Image src={Betelgeuse} alt="betelgeuse-gameplay"/>
